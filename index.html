About

Hello, my name is Daksha Joshi, and I am the owner and head stylist of Dax Beauty Salon & Spa. I started working in the beauty industry 15 years ago and have a wide variety of experience from traditional indian henna and bridal services to the most modern techniques in hair styling and skincare like the Brazillian Blowout and Keratin Treatments. I regularly attend advanced beauty education classes and trade shows to stay up to date on my craft and informed on the latest products and services in the industry. I work because happy clients make me happy.
